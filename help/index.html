Welcome to the track-B Help Center. Here, you'll find answers to frequently asked questions and information to assist you with using our service.
 Frequently Asked Questions
 Q: How do I track a bus in real-time?
A: To track a bus in real-time, simply enter your bus number or route into the search bar on our homepage. You'll be able to see the bus's current location on the map.
Q: What if my bus is delayed?
A: If your bus is delayed, we'll provide updates on the estimated arrival time. You can also sign up for notifications to receive alerts about delays and route changes.
